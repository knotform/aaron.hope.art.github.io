WHY KNOT ?


Knot theory is the mathematics field studying string shapes formed in things like our DNA strands, tied shoelaces, and rope. Mathematical knots are closed-loops that embed a circle in 3-d space.


Knot Forms are tensile suspended sculptures inspired by circular shapes found in nature. Through continuous curving surfaces, loops in space, symmetry, lightness, color, and imagery they contemplate our bonds with each other and the world we share.
